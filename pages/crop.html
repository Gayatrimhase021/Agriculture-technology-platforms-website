Cotton

Cotton is a major Kharif crop grown in black soil. It requires warm climate and moderate rainfall. Duration: 160-180 days. Major producing states: Maharashtra, Gujarat, Telangana.

Onion

Onion is grown in Rabi season. Requires well-drained loamy soil. Duration: 90-120 days. Major producing states: Maharashtra, Karnataka.

Soyabean

Soyabean is protein-rich Kharif crop. Requires medium rainfall and fertile soil. Duration: 100-120 days. Major producing states: Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra.

Sugarcane

Sugarcane is a long duration annual crop. Requires heavy irrigation and fertile soil. Duration: 10-12 months. Major producing states: Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra.
